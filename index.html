Rime team

Crafting games

Who are we?

Rime is an indie game development team based in Ekaterinburg, Russia.
We focus on making smart, yet simple and stylish games for mobile and desktop platforms.

Origin

Rime was founded by Aleksey "0xc0dec" Fedotov in the beginning of 2014. Before that he published several games that now can also be considered belonging to the Rime team.

Mission

Our mission is to deliver high quality, unconvensional games that should make people rethink the definition of the word "game". We believe a game is a new way of self expression, a tool for a game developer to share his feelings and emotions with the gamers.
