Line Bound

Guide the line on its way inside another line. Avoid obstacles and adopt the increasing speed. Choose correct movement directions depending on camera view angle. Improve you reaction, strengthen your nerves and never give up in this extreme minimalistic arcade game.

Controls

Tap on left screen half – rotate line left, tap on right screen half – rotate line right.

Featured soundtrack: Silence.
Platform: iOS, Android
Release date: August 15, 2013
